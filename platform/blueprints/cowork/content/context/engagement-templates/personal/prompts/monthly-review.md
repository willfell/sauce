# Monthly review — personal (PREVIOUS month)

Reviewing the PREVIOUS month. Compose using the gather outputs (finance full-month, cc-debt-snapshot monthly-close, calendar next-month, imessage 31-day, projects monthly, threads monthly-audit, forward-stressors). Body shape:

## 🗓️ Month in review

One 4–6 sentence paragraph naming the month's arc — what shifted, big decisions made, what carried forward.

## 💳 Finance month-close

This is the authoritative Credit Debt Payoff reconciliation moment. State the month's total spend, top 5 merchants, monthly paydown actual vs `debt_monthly_target_usd` (if configured), current CC balance, and a one-sentence trajectory call (on track / drift / breakthrough).

## 📆 Calendar next month

3–5 bullets naming next month's biggest commitments (gather-calendar horizon=next-month). Flag travel + multi-day commitments.

## 📊 Project status month

From gather-projects (monthly filter): projects closed this month · projects opened · in-progress longstanding. Each as a bullet with wikilink.

## 🧵 Threads monthly audit

From gather-threads monthly-audit: full audit of open threads (older than 30 days), still-active threads, auto-archive candidates. 5–8 bullets.

## 🌅 Forward stressors

Inline-scanned forward-look from `spice/trips/` (next 30–45 days) + annual bills in `spice/finance/budgets/` + explicit "planned purchase" notes. List specific stressors with rough dates. 3–6 bullets.

## ⏭️ Next month focus

One 3–5 sentence paragraph naming the next month's main objective. End with a concrete Day-1 starting point.

---

Tone: long view. This is the most retrospective of the five cadences — write it as if you're advising a friend on what to do next.
