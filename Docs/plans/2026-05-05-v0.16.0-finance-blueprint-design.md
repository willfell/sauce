# v0.16.0 Design — finance blueprint (workshop 0.15.0 → 0.16.0 MINOR)

> [!abstract] Goal
> Ship `finance@0.1.0` covering personal day-to-day (**Budgets** + **Paychecks**) AND business (**Invoices**) under one blueprint. Top-level Finance nav button + `beacon/finance/Finance.md` top hub. Three sub-areas isolated under their own sub-folders. Status pills (Planning/In Progress/Done) derived from frontmatter at render time — no manual user toggle.

---

## Source patterns

> [!info] Reference vaults
> - `headspace/Finance/{Paychecks,Budgets,Debt,Savings}/` — flat dated entity notes; rich frontmatter (`expenses[]`, `categories[planned, actual]`).
> - `ero/Finance/<YYYY-MM>/{Invoice, Time-Log, board}.md` — per-month folder ecosystem; auto-promote board cards via Templater. Mirrors trips/project per-entity pattern.
> - **Beacon synthesis:** ONE blueprint with three sub-areas. Headspace shape for personal (flat dated). ERO shape for business (per-month ecosystem).

---

## Architecture

> [!example]- Module layout (under `module_directory: "finance"`)
> ```
> beacon/finance/
> ├── Finance.md                              ← top hub (top-level nav button)
> ├── budgets/
> │   ├── Budgets.md                          ← area hub
> │   └── Budget-YYYY-MM.md                   ← entity (flat, one per month)
> ├── paychecks/
> │   ├── Paychecks.md
> │   └── Paycheck-YYYY-MM-DD.md              ← entity (filename = pay_period_start)
> └── invoices/
>     ├── Invoices.md
>     └── <YYYY-MM>/                          ← per-month ecosystem (mirrors trips/<slug>/)
>         ├── Invoice-YYYY-MM.md              ← invoice atlas
>         ├── Time-Log-YYYY-MM.md
>         └── board/
>             ├── Board-YYYY-MM.md            ← Kanban (auto-promote cards)
>             └── <CardName>/<CardName>.md    ← folder-style cards (Templater promotes)
> ```
>
> **Filename invariants** (gotcha 8):
> - Paycheck — `Paycheck-YYYY-MM-DD.md` (start_date suffix; date-routed)
> - Budget / Invoice — `<Name>-YYYY-MM.md` (month suffix)
> - Per-invoice ecosystem clones trips' `<slug>/board/<task>/` per-entity sub-folder pattern; lives within landmine #11 invariant (one module_directory; sub-folders are per-entity instances).

## Status derivation

> [!success] Derived (no manual toggle in v1)
> Helper class `FinanceStatus.derive(page, type) → {label, tone}`. Single source of truth used by every `*Cards` class.
>
> | Type | Done (green / `success`) | In Progress (yellow / `warn`) | Planning (neutral / `muted`) |
> |:----:|---|---|---|
> | **Budget** | month is past | month is current | month is future |
> | **Paycheck** | every `expenses[].paid == true` | today >= `pay_period_start` (any unpaid) | today < `pay_period_start` |
> | **Invoice** | `submitted_date` set | `hours > 0` (no submitted_date) | `hours == 0` |

## Top-level Finance.md hub composition

> [!example]- Top-down render order
> 1. Space nav buttons (registry-driven; includes new Finance entry)
> 2. **Action row** — three buttons: New Budget / New Paycheck / New Invoice
> 3. **Pending Budgets** section — `BudgetsCards` filtered to status != "done"
> 4. **Pending Paychecks** section — `PaychecksCards` filtered
> 5. **Pending Invoices** section — `InvoicesCards` filtered
>
> Per-area hubs (`Budgets.md`/`Paychecks.md`/`Invoices.md`) each render their own `New <X>` button + full `<X>Cards` listing (status="all"). Cheap symmetry.

> Invoice creation also scaffolds `Time-Log-YYYY-MM.md` + `board/Board-YYYY-MM.md` in same step (mirrors NewTripButton's multi-file scaffold).

## Open questions resolved (during brainstorming)

> [!info]- Decisions locked
> - **Manual status override?** NO for v1. Derived-only. CF if real-world edge case surfaces.
> - **Auto-promote shape?** Folder-style `<YYYY-MM>/board/<CardName>/<CardName>.md` (mirrors trips/project).
> - **Per-area hub action buttons?** YES (cheap symmetry; mirrors Trips.md hub having New Trip even though Finance.md does too).
> - **Three separate New buttons or one dispatcher?** Three separate (each tailored to its inputs; clearer UX).
> - **Paycheck filename format?** `Paycheck-YYYY-MM-DD.md` (start_date only; full period in frontmatter).
